About me.

Welcome to my website. My name's Eric Fallis, I'm a 19 year old photographer from Red Deer, Alberta. I started when I was a young boy in 2010 at just 12 years old and have since pursued it as a career. In 2015 I fell in love with watching nature, people, animals and making the graphics that came with that. I invite you to look through the galleries of my work if you're interested in commissioning me. You can get in contact by either using the form below or you can direct message me on Twitter.
